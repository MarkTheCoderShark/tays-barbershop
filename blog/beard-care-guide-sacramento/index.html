Ultimate Beard Care Guide for Sacramento Men

Professional grooming tips, product picks, and maintenance schedule

Healthy beards start with a simple routine. Our barbers share the steps Sacramento men can use to keep beards looking sharp between appointments.

Daily Routine

  • Wash 2–3x weekly with a gentle beard wash
  • Apply beard oil daily to hydrate hair and skin
  • Brush to distribute oils and train growth

Maintenance Schedule

  • Line-up: every 1–2 weeks
  • Trim/shape: every 3–4 weeks
  • Deep condition: monthly
